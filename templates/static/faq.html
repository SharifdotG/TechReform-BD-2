What payment methods do you accept?

At TechReform BD, we accept a variety of payment methods to provide you with maximum convenience:

  • All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Mobile banking options (bKash, Nagad, Rocket)
  • Bank transfers
  • Cash on delivery for selected areas

All online payments are secured with industry-standard encryption to ensure your financial information remains safe.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, TechReform BD primarily serves customers within Bangladesh. However, we do offer limited international shipping options to select countries.

For international orders:

  • Additional shipping fees will apply
  • Delivery times typically range from 7-21 business days
  • Import duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient
  • International warranty claims may require shipping back to Bangladesh

Please contact our customer service team before placing an international order to confirm availability, shipping costs, and estimated delivery time to your location.

How can I track my order?

Tracking your order with TechReform BD is simple and convenient:

  • Account Dashboard: Log into your account and navigate to "Order History" to see real-time status updates
  • Tracking Email: You'll receive an automated email with tracking information once your order ships
  • SMS Updates: If you provided a mobile number, you'll get SMS notifications about delivery status

Most orders ship within 24 hours of placement, and you can expect tracking information to be available within 1 business day after shipping.
